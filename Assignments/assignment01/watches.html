Audemars Piguet & Richard Mille - $40,799

Audemars Piguet and Richard Mille are luxury watch brands known for their high-end timepieces and innovative use of materials, including sapphire crystal. Both brands are revered for their exceptional craftsmanship and avant-garde designs.

Ulysse Nardin - $23,499

Ulysse Nardin is a Swiss luxury watch manufacturer known for its innovative designs and marine chronometers. The brand emphasizes craftsmanship and precision, offering a range of timepieces from classic to avant-garde styles.

Rolex - $7,899

Rolex is one of the most renowned Swiss watch brands globally, synonymous with luxury, prestige, and craftsmanship. Established in 1905, Rolex is celebrated for its iconic designs, precision movements, and robust durability.

Raymond Weil - $1,599

Raymond Weil is a Swiss watchmaker known for producing elegant and classic timepieces with a focus on traditional craftsmanship and contemporary design. The brand offers a range of collections suitable for both formal and casual occasions.

Citizen - $439

Citizen is a Japanese watch brand recognized for its affordable yet reliable timepieces. Founded in 1918, Citizen produces a wide range of watches, from quartz to eco-drive solar-powered models, catering to diverse tastes and budgets.

OMEGA - $4,599

OMEGA is a prestigious Swiss watchmaker renowned for its exceptional quality, innovation, and historical significance. Established in 1848, OMEGA has a rich heritage and is famous for producing the official timepieces of NASA and the Olympics.
