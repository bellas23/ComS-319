Canon EOS 6D Mark II - $1,399

With a 26.2-megapixel sensor and DIGIC 7 processor, the Mark II delivers high-quality images, even in low light. Its 45-point autofocus system ensures quick and accurate subject tracking. Built-in Wi-Fi, NFC, and Bluetooth allow for easy connectivity and remote control via smart devices, making this camera ideal for photographers seeking reliability and versatility in their equipment.

Canon EOS 850D - $999

The Canon EOS 850D is a versatile DSLR camera designed for enthusiasts and budding photographers. It has a 24.1-megapixel APS-C CMOS sensor and DIGIC 8 image processor for high-quality images, along with a 45-point autofocus system for fast and precise focusing. The camera features an optical viewfinder, vari-angle touchscreen LCD, supports 4K video recording and has built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity making it an excellent choice for photographers looking to elevate their skills.

Sony α7 III - $1,999

The Sony Alpha a7 III is a full-frame mirrorless camera known for its high-quality imaging, advanced autofocus, and sturdy construction. It features a 24.2-megapixel sensor, 693 phase-detection and 425 contrast-detection autofocus points, and 4K video recording capabilities. With 5-axis in-body image stabilization, it offers excellent performance for both photography and videography, making it a favored option among professionals and enthusiasts.

Sony αR - $3,499

The Sony Alpha R is a high-end mirrorless digital camera designed for professional photographers and enthusiasts alike. It boasts a full-frame sensor, 4K video resolution, and a wide range of compatible lenses. Whether capturing stunning landscapes, dynamic portraits, or fast-paced action, the Sony Alpha R delivers outstanding results, making it a top choice for discerning photographers seeking uncompromising quality and versatility.

FUJIFILM X-T4 - $1,700

The FUJIFILM X-T4 is a professional-grade mirrorless camera renowned for its exceptional image quality, advanced features, and robust build. With a 26.1MP sensor, 6.5-stop in-body image stabilization, and 4K video recording capabilities up to 60p, it offers versatility for both photographers and videographers. The X-T4's improved autofocus system, weather-sealed magnesium alloy body, vari-angle touchscreen LCD, and high-resolution electronic viewfinder further enhance its appeal.

Nikon FG - $150

First introduced in 1982, The Nikon FG is a 35mm film SLR camera that features a vertically traveling metal-bladed focal plane shutter with speeds ranging from 1/1000 to 1 second. It contains a bulb mode for longer exposures and has an aperture-priority automatic exposure mode as well as manual exposure control. The Nikon FG includes a through-the-lens (TTL) center-weighted light metering system for accurate exposure measurement.
